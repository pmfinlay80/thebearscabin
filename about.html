About the Bear

Designing and creating perfectly unique gifts is what The Bear’s Cabin love to do. All of my handcrafted pieces are made using unfinished wood, allowing me to select the perfect finishing touches for each individual item. I also have a growing gallery of unique, handmade box frame gifts. I do endeavour to support other local businesses where I can. While it’s not always possible, it is important to me to support rural communities as much as I can.

It started out in County Leitrim, Ireland, making little gifts for our families and friends at the kitchen table. It was exciting to be able to give handmade, unique gifts for special occasions. Then, in Winter 2017, a friend introduced me to a new type of crafting using a scroll saw. With a last minute Christmas gift request, things have not been the same since, including my utility room!

My little hobby started to change. I started to look for reasons to make things rather than waiting to make things for special occasions I was a part of. I realised that the skills I had already developed in other areas, like technology, could be used along with my new-found love of woodworking.

I truly love learning new things, testing myself with new ideas and enabling others to be excited about the gifts they are giving! It is so rewarding to be able to design something for someone, simple or complex, and be able to follow it through to end with the perfect finish. My hands do most of the work, accompanied by significant amounts of brain power from beginning to end.
